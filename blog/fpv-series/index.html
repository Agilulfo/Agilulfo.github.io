The FPV series

I've recently become fascinated with FPV (First-Person View) drones. This hobby is demanding and requires manual, technical, and piloting skills, along with plenty of practice. The steep learning curve and the sheer amount of information can often discourage newcomers.

This series of articles is the resource I wish I had when I started. It aims to provide a solid foundation of knowledge, so you can keep building your skills without feeling overwhelmed. We won't be diving deep into specific technical aspects. Instead, we'll focus on the essential information you need to get started.

This post will also serve as the index for the entire series.
